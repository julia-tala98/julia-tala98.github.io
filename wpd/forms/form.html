This form functions by giving the form tag an id of signup. CSS can be written just for this id that will change the look of the entire form such as setting a max-width, padding, and a background. Different classes such as "legend" and "fieldset" are assigned so that the sizing and overall appearance can be manipulated of each individual section of the form. Also, the text withing the id can be given a size depending on their class. For the "select" tag all browsers are given the appearance of "none" so that new css can be written and the form can look better then the default appearance. SVG images are put in place for the radio and checkbox buttons, putting in place a new picture that shifts when selected. This gives the appearance of a box being checked or unchecked, even though it is all one picture.
